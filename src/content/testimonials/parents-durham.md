We engaged with Michael to carry out a report for our son, hoping to gain a detailed understanding of his strengths and weaknesses so we could support his development. Michael observed our son on two separate occasions, as we wanted to see how he performed in different game settings. The way Michael handled the process was first class — he operated discreetly, ensuring our child had no idea he was being watched. We felt this approach gave us the most honest and accurate picture of our son as a player.

The report Michael produced was incredible. The level of detail was outstanding, and the structure made it easy to follow and put into action. It was completely unbiased and honest, and what surprised us most was how clearly our son's character came through. After only two games, you would think Michael had known him far longer.

The analysis of strengths and weaknesses was clear, constructive, and supported by a four-week development plan that we were able to implement immediately. We especially valued the separate parent and player sections. Our son could read about his strengths and areas for improvement in a way written specifically for him. The "bottle checklist" was a brilliant addition — simple, snappy, and something he now refers to during games, exactly as intended, helping him refocus before, during, and after matches.

Another standout feature was the parents' checklist for what to say after a game. When emotions run high, it's the perfect reminder for any parent.

We would absolutely recommend Michael to any parent with a player at any level who wants honest, professional guidance on how to help their child improve. He is a total professional who provides far more than just a report. The work he puts in beforehand to understand the player, the depth and clarity of the report itself, and the ongoing support he offers afterwards are truly outstanding.

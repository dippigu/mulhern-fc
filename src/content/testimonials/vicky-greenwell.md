I worked with Michael Mulhern while studying for my UEFA A Licence and also took part in a number of workshops. The support was thoughtful, challenging, and grounded in real coaching practice rather than theory alone.

The mentoring process encouraged reflection, ownership, and a deeper understanding of learning environments in football. That approach had a significant impact on how I developed as a coach and stayed with me as I progressed into professional roles, including coaching at WSL level.

I would strongly recommend this work to coaches and organisations who are serious about developing people, not just programmes.

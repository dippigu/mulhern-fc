Michael has been working with my son Seb (18), who is aiming to progress at a professional level, and the experience has been extremely positive.

What stands out is how tailored each session is to the individual. Not just technically, but mentally as well. Michael also played an important role in helping Seb prepare for a trial with a professional club, giving him both structure and confidence going into that environment.

Seb has benefitted from Michael’s structured approach. The sessions are built around realistic match scenarios, with a strong attention to detail, particularly around technique, body positioning, and using both feet, which you don’t often get in standard team training.

The written feedback/reports are another standout aspect. They are clear, detailed, and genuinely useful, breaking down strengths, areas for development, and providing a plan going forward. The emphasis on psychological preparation and visualisation has also helped improve Seb’s confidence.

Most importantly, Seb really enjoys the sessions. He comes away motivated, focused, and with a clear sense of what he is working towards.

Michael combines real expertise with a genuine investment in the player, and his coaching has made a meaningful difference to Seb’s development.

My son is a high level U13 goalkeeper. He kept asking me what areas he could improve on to try and get his chance to push into an academy. Being a parent with little knowledge in goalkeeping, I couldn't answer this. So after finding out about Michael Mulhern Football Consultancy, I got in touch with Michael who found out more about Kai. We all agreed that to get full knowledge of Kai's possible improvement areas it would be good to get a report on three matches rather than just one.

After doing this and finding out the areas Kai needed to improve on, Michael wrote a full detailed report which also included a player and parent pack. After reading this and spending a classroom session with Michael, my son has massively improved on the areas that needed tweaked.

We still have a way to go to get to academy level but Michael and my son meet up or chat weekly to improve on these areas. This has by far been the best decision in his football journey because we now know exactly what areas we need to work on.

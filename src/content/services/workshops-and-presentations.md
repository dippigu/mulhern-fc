These are conversations, not lectures. Football is globally recognised, cross-generational and genuinely felt as a subject — and these workshops treat it accordingly. Sessions are built around curiosity, shared cultural interest and honest discussion. Participants are invited to bring their own knowledge, memories and questions. There is no qualification at the end. There does not need to be.

Alongside the discussion workshops, the same approach is offered to clubs, academies, schools and individual coaches — through targeted training workshops and one-to-one coach mentorship. Coaching content is shaped around the group, from player development and talent identification to session design and communication with players and parents.

Pathways are rarely about a list of clubs and a hopeful email. They are about who knows the player, who picks up the phone, and whether the introduction comes from someone the destination already trusts.

This is a service built on relationships across professional and international football, used by players seeking the right next step, by families considering an international move, and by clubs, universities and academies looking for credible candidates. Every placement is bespoke, so there are no standard packages. Get in touch to discuss what you need.

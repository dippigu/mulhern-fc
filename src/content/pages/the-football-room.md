These are not lectures. They are conversations — structured around football topics that matter, led by someone who has lived inside the professional game at international level for more than twenty years.

Every session is built around curiosity, shared cultural interest and honest discussion. Participants are invited to bring their own knowledge, memories and questions. There is no qualification at the end. There does not need to be.

Football is globally recognised, cross-generational and genuinely felt as a subject. These workshops treat it accordingly.

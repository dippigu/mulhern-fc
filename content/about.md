About Michael 
I am a UEFA A Licence Football (soccer) coach with more than two decades of experience across elite women's International football, the Women's Super League (WSL), and the professional men's game in the United Kingdom.

 

My career includes coaching the England Women's U-23's, and head coach at both Sunderland and Middlesbrough Women.

 

I was a scout for the England Women's Senior Team and the girls and women's pathway from U-17 -U-23. I also worked at Newcastle United and for the English Football Association as a coach mentor.

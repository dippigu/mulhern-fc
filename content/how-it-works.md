How It Works 
A clear and simple process designed to provide honest, development-focused football assessment for players, families and institutions.  Everything is delivered with transparency, professionalism and care.

1. Send Your Footage 
You send your match video link (Veo, YouTube, Hudl etc). I review the footage to ensure it is clear enough for assessment.

2. Assessment Begins 
I complete a full analysis of the match, assessing technical, tactical, physical and behavioural elements, before producing a detailed written report.

3. Receive Your Report 
Your report is delivered digitally. Depending on the service chosen, you may also receive a Zoom consultation and a full development plan.

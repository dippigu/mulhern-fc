School Partnerships 
I partner with International and UK-based schools to provide independent, elite-standard football evaluation and development support for students. Schools use these services to support pathways, applications, player development and programme quality.

What Schools Receives 
•Independent assessment for students 

​

•Written evaluation reports

​

•Development priorities and guidance 

​

•Support with pathways (UK, USA and International)

​

•Zoom meetings with students, parents or staff

​

•Workshops for staff or students

​

•Priority access and faster turnaround

 

•Term, season or annual support options

Schools may choose from:

 

•Assessment Package - A set number of evaluations per term

​

•Placement Support Package - Evaluation + pathway guidance + reporting 

​

•Full School Partnership - Year-round support, workshops, priority access and development planning
